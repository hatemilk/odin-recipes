Risotto

This is Kenji's scientific approach to the world's greatest Risotto. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Fresh Parsley
  • 1/4 Cup Fresh tarragon leaves
  • 6 Cups Vegetable Stock or Water
  • 2 Cups Spinach
  • 4 Scallions, finely chopped
  • 1/4 Cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 1/2 Cups Risotto style rice
  • 2 medium garlic cloves
  • 2 Table spoons vegetable oil
  • 8 oz mixed wild mushrooms
  • 1 small shallot minced
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • Kosher Salt and Fresh Ground Pepper

Steps

  1. Finely chop 1 tbs each or parsley and tarragon. Cover with a damp paper towel and refrigerate.
  2. Boil stock and prepare a large ice bath. Add spinach, remaining parsley and tarragon, and scallion greens to boiling water. Cook for 30 seconds, then add to ice bath.
  3. Trransfer blanched greens to blender and blend with 1/2 cup of stock.
  4. Heat olive oil in skillet. Add rice and cook until golden, then add scallions and garlic. Cook for 1min
  5. Add all but 1 1/2 cups of stock to the skillet, cover and reduce heat to lowest setting. Cook rice for 10 minutes undisturbed. Stir and cook for 10 minutes longer.
  6. Cook mushrooms in a pan with vegetable oil until well browned. Add shallot and cook 30 sec more. Add 1/4 cup of stock, lemon juice and soy sauce. Remove from hear and season to taste.
  7. Add remaining broth to risotto. Turn heat to high and cook until thick. Off the heat, stir in green puree, lemon zest and parsley + tarragon. Top with mushrooms and serve.
